About Uday Bharat Mission

Uday Bharat Mission is a social initiative committed to building a self-reliant, empowered, and dignified society by addressing the root causes of begging and addiction.

Our Key Projects

From Freedom from Begging to a Self-Reliant Society

This project focuses on rehabilitating individuals involved in begging by providing skill development, vocational training, and employment opportunities.

  • Skill training & livelihood programs
  • Education and awareness initiatives
  • Social reintegration and dignity restoration

From Freedom from Addiction to a Self-Reliant Society

This initiative aims to help individuals overcome substance addiction through counseling, rehabilitation, and long-term support systems.

  • De-addiction awareness programs
  • Counseling and rehabilitation support
  • Post-recovery skill development

How We Help People

Providing skills for sustainable income
Restoring dignity and self-confidence
Reducing social dependency
Encouraging responsible and healthy living
Building stronger families and communities
Creating long-term social transformation
